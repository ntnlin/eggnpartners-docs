1) 3-Tier Tokenomics Architecture

To simultaneously satisfy the stability requirements of institutional investors and the clear utility experience of general users, the ecosystem is meticulously categorized into three functional tiers:

  • Tier 1: NFT (Access & Identity Layer): A strategic gateway for entering real-world assets. Beyond simple imagery, it incorporates membership tiers, proof of ownership, and governance access rights integrated with DID (Decentralized Identity).
  • Tier 2: RWA Token (Reward & Yield Layer): A layer where the value of the real economy is directly projected. These act as yield-bearing certificates, distributing actual cash flows — such as mobility operation revenues from EHang (NASDAQ) or K-Pop IP royalties from Danal (KOSDAQ) — to token holders.
  • Tier 3: EGGT (Utility & Currency Layer): The native currency acting as the "lifeblood" of the ecosystem. It is utilized for gas fees, governance staking (EGGTcourt), primary payment for RWA assets, and as a core medium for liquidity provision.

2) S2EXT2E Reward Engine: Anti-Manipulation Mathematical Logic

To ensure the long-term solvency of the reward pool and prevent exploitation by Sybil actors or automated bots, EGGT utilizes a Non-Linear Multi-Factor Algorithm.

Formula: Individual Reward Distribution (Ri)

$$R_i = r \times \left[ \frac{\log(1+s_i) \times \sqrt{x_i}}{\displaystyle\sum \log(1+s) \times \sum \sqrt{x}} \right]$$
  • r: Total protocol-allocated reward pool for the specific epoch.
  • s: Social Impact Factor (Quantified SNS contribution verified via DID-linked metadata).
  • x: Liquidity Contribution Index (Verified on-chain asset trading volume).

By applying logarithmic and square root functions, the engine suppresses the influence of "Whales" and bots, ensuring that rewards are distributed across a healthy, active contributor base while maintaining price floor stability.

3) R-Engine: Automated Protocol Revenue Distribution

The R-Engine is a smart-contract-enforced settlement module that automates the redistribution of protocol fees. This precludes any subjective or centralized intervention in the "Real Yield" sequence.

Formula: Reward Distribution by R-Engine (RA)

$$R_A = \left( \frac{K_i \times M_i}{K_T} \right) \times n$$
  • Ki / KT: Individual vs. Total Staking Weight.
  • n: Total verified reward resources accumulated in the R-Engine.
  • Mi: Exponential Time-Weight Multiplier (Increases with staking duration to reward long-term holders).

Staking in EGGT is not a passive lock-up but a Productive Participation in the RWA economy. This yield is backed by Real Economic Flow, satisfying the most rigorous institutional standards for sustainable finance.

4) Integrity & Anti-Manipulation: Automated Value Defense

To maintain the economic equilibrium of the RWA Empire, two advanced automated protocols operate continuously:

  • EGGT Exchanger (Automatic Buy-Pressure Protocol): When RWA transactions are executed using external assets (ETH, MATIC, KLAY, etc.), the system automatically swaps a designated percentage of the fees for EGGT on the open market. This volume flows into the R-Engine, creating a continuous organic buy-back effect and buy pressure for EGGT regardless of the payment currency.
  • Sybil Defense & Filtering Logic: To protect the reward pool, the S2EXT2E algorithm applies strict active filtering. Accounts falling below the threshold for both activity index (s) and transaction index (x) within a specific period (Epoch) are automatically excluded from distribution. This technical device ensures incentives are concentrated only on actual contributors and active RWA holders.

5) Omnichain Sovereignty & Strategic Liquidity via BSC

To ensure seamless cross-chain interoperability and meet the rigorous listing requirements of global top-tier exchanges, EGGT is issued via the BNB Smart Chain (BSC) as the primary liquidity layer. While leveraging the high-performance consensus and low-fee environment of BSC, the total supply is strictly capped at 1,000,000,000 EGGT across all integrated networks. This approach maintains the original fixed supply from the legacy ERC-20 standard without any inflationary dilution. By deploying as an Omnichain Fungible Token (OFT) using LayerZero technology, EGGT can move fluidly across diverse blockchain ecosystems, providing the deep liquidity required for institutional-grade trading and global exchange compatibility.

6) 'Analyzer' Mechanism and Fee Recirculation

External NFT data tracked by NCR (Standardless) technology is integrated into the ecosystem through the EGGT Analyzer, which serves as a decentralized oracle gateway for external NFT and RWA data. Small fees generated during metadata synchronization and value appraisal are paid in EGGT and 100% re-injected into the R-Engine, serving as a financial buffer that secures continuous reward resources even during periods of stagnant primary market trading.
